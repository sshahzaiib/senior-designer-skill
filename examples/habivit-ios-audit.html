I graded my own habit tracker against a 59-point design checklist. It failed. Most of the failures trace to one decision: every font size is hardcoded, so the type never scales. Silent VoiceOver is a close second.

The one decision

Every piece of text is set with a fixed .font(.system(size: N)) — sizes run from 40 pt down to 7 pt, with zero Dynamic Type anywhere in the app.

That single choice fails eight checklist items (T1, T4, T6, S1, S5, S6, plus C1 and S11): the text can't scale, and low-vision users get nothing. Fix it and roughly a third of the red turns green at once.
